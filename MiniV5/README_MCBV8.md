\# MCBV8



\## Overview

MCBV8 improves retrieval quality and handling of structured content such as tables, schedules, and factual rows.



\## Main changes from MCBV7

\- improved PDF extraction strategy

\- stronger chunking for structured content

\- better retrieval precision

\- cleaner citations

\- stronger handling of factual/table-like questions



\## Improvement focus

MCBV8 was designed to reduce weak retrieval and improve answers for structured handbook content.
